How it works

Three steps to your first swap.

Step 01

List what you can offer

Add the skills your team can spare — design hours, growth time, engineering sprints. You set the depth and availability.

Step 02

Match with a counterpart

Browse listings or get matched. Every team on Bartr is offering hours back, so the question is what they can trade.

Step 03

Lock the swap, ship the work

Scope each side, agree on terms, work async. We handle the contracts, hours-tracking, and dispute resolution.
